Foods to Include
  • Lean Proteins: Chicken, Fish, Eggs, Tofu, Lentils
  • Complex Carbs: Oats, Quinoa, Brown Rice, Sweet Potato
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, Nuts, Olive Oil, Salmon
  • Vegetables: Broccoli, Spinach, Kale, Bell Peppers
  • Fruits: Berries, Bananas, Apples, Oranges
Foods to Limit/Avoid
  • Processed Foods: Fast food, Packaged snacks
  • Sugary Drinks: Soda, Energy drinks, Sweetened juices
  • Refined Carbs: White bread, Pastries, Cookies
  • Trans Fats: Fried foods, Margarine
  • Excessive Alcohol: High in empty calories
