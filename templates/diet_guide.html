Proper nutrition is essential for achieving your fitness goals

Breakfast Options

  • Oatmeal with fruits & nuts
  • Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast
  • Greek yogurt with berries
  • Protein smoothie
  • Whole grain cereal with milk

Lunch Options

  • Grilled chicken with rice
  • Tuna sandwich with salad
  • Quinoa bowl with vegetables
  • Lentil soup with bread
  • Whole wheat pasta with lean meat

Dinner Options

  • Baked fish with vegetables
  • Chicken stir-fry with brown rice
  • Lean beef with sweet potato
  • Vegetable curry with rice
  • Grilled turkey with quinoa

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily. Increase intake during workouts.

Healthy Snacks:

  • Mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • Fresh fruits (apple, banana)
  • Protein bars
  • Vegetable sticks with hummus
  • Low-fat cheese

Foods to Avoid:

  • Processed foods
  • Sugary drinks
  • Fried foods
  • Excessive salt
  • Alcohol
